How to Export Your WhatsApp Chat

It takes less than 30 seconds. Follow the steps for your phone.

Android

1

Open WhatsApp and go to the chat you want to analyze

2

Tap the three dots (⋮) in the top right

3

Tap "More" → "Export chat"

4

Choose "Without Media" (the app only needs the text)

5

Share the .txt file to yourself (via email, Google Drive, or any app)

6

Upload that .txt file here

iPhone

1

Open WhatsApp and go to the chat you want to analyze

2

Tap the contact name or group name at the top

3

Scroll down and tap "Export Chat"

4

Choose "Without Media"

5

Share the .zip or .txt file to yourself (via email, Files app, etc.)

6

Upload that file here — both .zip and .txt are supported

lock

Works with both personal chats and group chats. Your chat is analyzed in memory and automatically deleted after 1 hour.
